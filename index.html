History

Antarctica has a frozen landscape that has inspired many expeditions into its farthest reaches. Create memories on your own expedition to Antarctica

Top 3 Expeditions to Antarctica

  1. Ann Chapman first woman led expedition in 1971 to research frozen lakes
  2. Rold Admunsen in 1910 was the first to reach the exact south pole
  3. Ernest Shakelton and his crew in 1914 set sail to be the first to traverse the Antarctic continent from shore to shore
